Game Play

Important Notes

  • OPEN CHAT ROOM- Everyone has to make sure to open chat room as it will be a medium to “hands up” to guess the song. 
  • STANDBY FOR DJ TO PLAY SONG - Listen carefully to the song that will be played by DJ
  • HANDS UP- Once DJ stop the song, mention group name in the chat room. The que advisor will pick who is the fastest in the chat room.
  • CONTINUE THE LYRIS - The rep from group will continue to sing the song for at least 2 lines, give the name of the song and the singer. 
  • ERRK.. WRONG LYRICS -  If the rep gets wrong, their chance will be opened to the other groups in the battle.
  • NO ONE GOT IT RIGHT -If still get wrongs, the battle considers done and will go to the next battle. 
  • PLEASE MUTE WHEN DJ PLAYS THE SONG - If anyone in the battles sing the song/try to guess the song/mention group name in chat room; before the song stopped, the song will be disqualified, and DJ will play the next song. 
  • THE FINALS - After all battles completed all 3 rounds, Top 3 will be selected based on scoring. Top 3 will have 3 round of battles. Rules are same but a bit challenges.  Champion will be selected based on the highest scoring.
